How to Play

Click Summon Hero to roll the dice and recruit characters to your Vanguard. The rarity of the character determines their Vitality (HP), Power (STR), and value.

Ascension

If you collect 10 duplicates of the exact same hero, an Ascend (10x) button will appear next to them in the Vanguard. Sacrificing 10 copies grants a permanent +1 Power boost to that hero class!

Equipment & Artifacts

Defeating dungeon bosses may reward you with Artifacts. Click the purple Equip Artifact button on any hero in your Vanguard to permanently assign an artifact and massively multiply their stats.

Boss Fights

Enter the Boss Lair and select up to 3 heroes from your Vanguard to face terrible foes. If your combined Power is high enough, you'll slay the boss and earn massive Gold bounties. If you lose, your heroes will safely retreat to fight another day.

Prestige (Rebirth)

Once you defeat the Final Boss (Floor 7) or unlock 100% of the Hero Index, a hidden Prestige button will appear at the top. Prestiging wipes your current run but permanently doubles ALL future gold income globally!
